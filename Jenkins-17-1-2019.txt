Email Notifications
=========================

2 Go to the Development job---->click on configure
3 Go to Post build actions--->Add post build action
4 Click on Email notifications
5 Enter the email ids of the team members seperated by space
6 Apply--->save
7 Open the dashboard of jenkins
8 Click on Manage Jenkins
